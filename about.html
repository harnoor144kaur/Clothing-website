About us

Get to know us

OUR BRAND

Welcome to the world of Littlebox, where we aim to offer quality, affordable and inspiring clothing for the young woman who wants to look good and have fun with her look.As our customer, you are at the heart of everything we do.

We aspire to provide you with the best online shopping experience while we adhere to the concept of “Defining your own fashion moment”.

Littlebox exists for the girl who is carefree in her attitude yet sweet in her charms with an ever-evolving and glowing personality.

Product Design

We create looks designed by in-house talent, made to equip millennial women with the fashion they need for all elements of their life. It all starts with an idea, conceived by the designer, keeping in mind the bold and strong spirit of our customers.

Manufacturing

We value quality and thus all our products are crafted completely and solely in INDIA. We take great pride in calling ourselves an authentic Indian brand, providing employment to local community with favoured female workforce.

Quality Assurance

After rigorous testing and inspection, our products are made into something we can be proud of a fashionable and reliable item! Our dedicated Quality Assurance Team follows a 5-Step Quality Check process with every piece of garment to make sure that only the best of the best reaches you.
